Executive Summary

The STEAM Diploma is Pyramakerz Technologies' comprehensive 80-hour professional development program designed to transform educators into confident, capable STEAM instructors. It blends theory, hands-on technology projects, and 21st-century pedagogy — covering everything from 3D printing and Arduino programming to Python, mobile app development, and web design. Graduates leave equipped to inspire the next generation of innovators.

Objection Handling Cheat Sheet
"I have no STEAM experience."
That's exactly who we designed this for. The program starts from first principles and builds up progressively — with support tools and dedicated coordinators every step of the way.
"80 hours is a big commitment."
Sessions are structured, spaced out, and supported by self-paced recordings — so trainees never fall behind. The diploma is designed to fit into an educator's schedule.
"What if I miss a session?"
Trainees can miss up to 2 sessions (15% absence allowed). All sessions are recorded and accessible in the digital library.
"Is this just theory?"
Far from it. 74 out of 80 hours are interactive. Trainees build functional projects — apps, circuits, 3D designs, smart systems — throughout the program.
